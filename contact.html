You can only claim your VAT back if we have shipped your goods directly to the VAT exempt country. To claim your refund, please contact out customer service team.

We will refund or exchange any unused items that are in a saleable condition within 30 days of purchase with the original store or gift receipt, or the order confirmation email, at the prices shown. Refunds will be processed to the original payment method shown on your store or gift receipt, or the order confirmation email.
